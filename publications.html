Publications

Welcome to my publications section! Delve into some of my contributions to the scientific community published in reputable journals. These works represent not just milestones in my academic career but also a passion for exploration, discovery, and the pursuit of understanding. Should you have any questions or suggestions, please don't hesitate to reach out to me.

Geodiversity Mapping & correlation

Geosciences Journal, 2022

Based on the results of my Bachelor's thesis, this article aimed to correlate geodiversity maps with other nature and anthropic elements in a mountainous region in Rio de Janeiro (Brazil) to discuss their support for land-use and nature conservation management and studies.

Geodiversity Mapping

Geoheritage Journal, 2020

Based on the results of my Bachelor's thesis, this article aimed to investigate the application of geodiversity maps in land management studies through a quantitative mapping of geodiversity using GIS tools in a mountainous region in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

Geodiversity and Urban Growth Mapping

Applied Geography Journal, 2017

This work aimed to assess the urban growth impacts on the geodiversity of Armação dos Búzios municipality, a coastal region in Rio de Janeiro (Brazil), using GIS tools.

Forest fires impacts on geoheritage/h2>

International Journal of Geoheritage and parks, 2024

Article based on my Master's thesis about the assessment of the potential impacts of forest fires on the geoheritage of Estrela UNESCO Global Geopark, Portugal, using GIS software and tools.
